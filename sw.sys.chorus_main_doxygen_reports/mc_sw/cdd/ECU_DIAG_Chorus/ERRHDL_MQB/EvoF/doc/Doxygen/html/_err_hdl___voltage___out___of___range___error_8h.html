Std_ReturnType ErrBatteryVoltageOutOfRange ( void  )

This function handle battery out of range errors.

Battery has three states(E_NORMAL_VOLTAGE, E_UNDER_VOLTAGE, E_OVER_VOLTAGE). For each of the states appropriate DTCs and DEM condition state should be set.

  • less then 5.5V - Under voltage
  • 6v to 18v - Normal voltage
  • above 18.5v - over voltage
